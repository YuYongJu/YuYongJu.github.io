AI-Powered SEC Filing Analytics Platform

A full-stack, enterprise-grade platform providing verified, citation-backed answers to financial analyst queries using Retrieval-Augmented Generation, XBRL cross-validation, and hybrid semantic search. 04 — PubMed Topic Modeling

LLM-Augmented Topic Modeling in Radiation Oncology

An end-to-end NLP pipeline analyzing 4,277 medical abstracts from the International Journal of Radiation Oncology using BERTopic, PubMedBERT embeddings, and a novel two-phase GPT validation framework. Maps research trends across a full decade (2013–2023) of radiation oncology literature.

Surfaced 101 candidate topics for clinician review through a self-validated GPT-4o-mini scoring framework, with 14 interactive Altair visualizations and a reproducible validation pipeline. Analyses contributed to the lab's ASTRO 2026 submission cycle (American Society for Radiation Oncology).
