01 — Project Overview

Sentrix enables organizations to plan autonomous drone missions, process live and recorded video feeds through computer vision pipelines, generate real-time alerts on detected anomalies, and produce actionable inspection reports. The platform is drone-agnostic with hardware adapters for DJI, Skydio, and MAVLink protocols.

At its core is the Autonomous Decision Engine (ADE) — a proprietary system with 5 graduated autonomy levels that governs what actions the AI can take autonomously vs. what requires human approval. A 6-factor risk scoring engine evaluates every proposed action in real-time, weighing tool category, reversibility, resource cost, operational context, time of day, and action frequency.

Built as a hybrid modular monolith (Next.js app + Python Vision AI service) with multi-tenant data isolation, real-time WebSocket communication, full audit trails for SOC2/ISO 27001 compliance, and 50+ UI views covering dashboards, maps, fleet management, camera feeds, analytics, and enterprise administration.

03 — Architecture
Core System

Autonomy Levels

5 graduated levels governing AI decision authority, from passive monitoring to emergency override protocols.

  • L1: Observer — monitor only
  • L2: Advisor — suggest, human confirms
  • L3: Supervised — auto low-risk
  • L4: Autonomous — full auto
  • L5: Emergency — safety override
Decision Engine

Risk Scoring

Every proposed AI action is evaluated in real-time against 6 weighted factors before execution or escalation.

  • Tool Category: 30% weight
  • Reversibility: 15% weight
  • Resource Cost: 15% weight
  • Operational Context: 15% weight
  • Time of Day: 10% weight
  • Action Frequency: 15% weight

04 — Autonomy Levels
L1
Observer
Monitor and log only. No autonomous actions taken. All detections recorded for human review.
L2
Advisor
Suggest actions with reasoning. Human operator must confirm every decision before execution.
L3
Supervised
Execute low-risk actions autonomously. Medium and high-risk actions escalated to human operator.
L4
Autonomous
Full autonomous operation within policy bounds. Actions logged and auditable in real-time.
L5
Emergency
Override normal protocols for safety-critical situations. Immediate action with post-event review.
